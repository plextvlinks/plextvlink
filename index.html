How to Link Your TV to Plex:

1. Download the Plex App

Find it in your TV’s store:
LG 📦 Samsung 📱 Android TV 🟢 Apple TV 🍏 Roku 🟣 Amazon Fire TV 🔥

2. Open the App & Select "Sign In";

New to Plex? Tap "Sign Up" first ✏️

3. Get Your 4-Character Code

You’ll see a code on your TV screen (like ABCD-EFGH) — keep this visible.

4. On Your Phone or Computer:

Head to plex.tv/link 📱💻 Log in if prompted Enter the code from your TV Click "Continue" ✅

🎉 You're All Set!

Your TV will refresh automatically and your Plex library will be ready to explore.

Having Trouble?:

Code frozen? Refresh the Plex app on your TV 🌐 Code disappeared? Reopen the Plex app ⏳ No connection? Double-check your Wi-Fi/router 📶 Need more help? Visit Plex Support
